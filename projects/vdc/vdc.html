I founded Synaesthetic Software in 2002 to promote my invention, the Virtual Drum Clinic. The Virtual Drum Clinic aims to accelerate the learning of technical and conceptual aspects of drum performance. The software is made up of three interlocking components: an interactive 3-dimensional visualization of music notation; an adaptive feedback system that generates custom lessons by identifying a user's weaknesses in performance; and a composition editor that can record highly accurate transcriptions of performance, and which facilitates the creation of polyrhythmic and/or algorithmic music.

The patent application and associated images are available online at the United States Patent & Trademark Office website, by searching for patent number 20040237756.
